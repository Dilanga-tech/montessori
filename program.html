Infants overview

Our infant program is designed to provide a secure and nurturing environment for the youngest members of our community. Our experienced teachers focus on individualized care, promoting a sense of trust and security. Infants' fine and gross motor skills are reinforced with tactile and sensory experiences, developmentally appropriate art projects and literary stimulation with visualization.

Toddlers overview

Toddlers at Bassett Creek Montessori engage in play-based learning activities that promote socialization, language development and motor skills. Our program encourages exploration and discovery in a safe and supportive setting.

Preschoolers overview

Preschoolers embark on a journey of structured yet creative learning experiences. Our curriculum introduces foundational skills in language, math, science and the arts, preparing them for a seamless transition to elementary education.

At Bassett Creek Montessori we believe that learning should be both fun and meaningful. Our educational activities are carefully curated to encourage critical thinking, problem solving and a lifelong love for learning. Through play based learning and creative experiences children develop not only academic skills but also social and emotional intelligence.
