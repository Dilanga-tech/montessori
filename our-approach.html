Our Approach

Philosophy & Approach

Bassett Creek Montessori follows the renowned Montessori philosophy, emphasizing hands-on learning, individualized instruction, and fostering a sense of independence. Our approach focuses on providing a stimulating environment that encourages curiosity, creativity and a love for learning.

Our curriculum is designed to instill a love for learning and encourage exploration. Learning is seamlessly integrated into daily activities, allowing children to discover and understand the world around them at their own pace.

Give Your Child the Best Start at Bassett Creek Montessori!

At Bassett Creek Montessori, we nurture curiosity, independence, and a love of learning in every child. Our experienced educators guide your child through a personalized learning journey designed to spark creativity and growth.
