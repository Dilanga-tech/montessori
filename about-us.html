About Us

Introduction and Overview

Welcome to Bassett Creek Montessori, located in Golden Valley, MN, a place where curiosity meets creativity, and young minds flourish. We are dedicated to providing a nurturing and enriching environment that fosters a love for learning from the very start. Our commitment is to create a community where each child's unique potential is recognized and celebrated.

To empower children to thrive in life through self-lead and self-motivated development. Creating self-sufficient skills for a resilient and confident individual.

We keep each child engaged in learning through their own curiosity and exploration of the world. Each unique individual contributes to the learning experience of the whole community and together we build our minds to prepare for a lifetime of learning and growing into productive and capable members of society, ready to thrive in elementary education and beyond.

We value each child's independent learning journey, emphasized by dramatic play and age-appropriate activity plans directed to each child's stage of development. We encourage quality learning experiences through interactive play and educational lessons that are taught by our attentive and well-trained teachers. Keeping in mind what Maria Montessori said, “Our care of the child should be governed, not by the desire to make him learn things, but by the endeavor always to keep burning within him that light which is called intelligence.”

Give Your Child the Best Start at Bassett Creek Montessori!

At Bassett Creek Montessori, we nurture curiosity, independence, and a love of learning in every child. Our experienced educators guide your child through a personalized learning journey designed to spark creativity and growth.
